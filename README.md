# Atarax

A minimalist Stoic journaling web app designed to cultivate clarity, discipline, and inner calm.

## Overview

Atarax is a guided Stoic journaling tool inspired by the philosophy of Marcus Aurelius, Seneca, and Epictetus.  
It helps users reflect on their thoughts and actions through structured prompts for morning intention, midday awareness, and evening examination.

The goal of the project is to combine Stoic philosophy with modern digital journaling to help users build mental clarity and resilience.

## Features

• Morning intention prompts  
• Midday reflection check-ins  
• Evening philosophical examination  
• Weekly and monthly reflection pages  
• Stoic quote and reading list section  
• Reflection board for deeper thinking

## Why I Built This

As a student interested in philosophy, productivity, and technology, I wanted to build a tool that integrates Stoic principles with modern digital journaling.

Atarax is designed to help people slow down, reflect intentionally, and cultivate tranquility (ataraxia) through structured thought.
